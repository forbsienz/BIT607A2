Dog Care Education

Best practices for grooming your dog at home.

Maintaining your dog's grooming routine between salon visits is crucial for their health and happiness.

  • Brushing: Regular brushing prevents painful matting and distributes natural oils across their coat. Short-haired dogs benefit from weekly brushing, while long-haired breeds may need it daily.
  • Nail Care: If you can hear your dog's nails clicking on the floor, they are too long! Long nails can affect their posture and joint health.
  • Ear Health: Check your dog's ears weekly for redness, bad odors, or excess wax, which can be signs of infection.
  • Dental Hygiene: Just like humans, dogs get plaque and tartar buildup. Use pet-safe toothpaste to brush their teeth regularly to prevent dental disease.
